Our Mission

To democratize access to land and sustainable living resources by creating an integrated ecosystem that combines a Community Land Trust, digital platform, and holistic support services.

🌟

Our Vision

A Region IV-A where every motivated individual has access to affordable land, knowledge, and community support to live a regenerative, self-sufficient lifestyle.

Our Core Values

🌱

Regeneration

Healing the land through sustainable agriculture following RA 10068.

🤝

Community

Building resilient, connected neighborhoods.

⚖️

Accessibility

Removing financial barriers to land ownership.

📚

Education

Empowering through hands-on workshops.

🌾

Food Security

Promoting local food production.

Our Journey

2023 - Research Phase: Identified fragmentation problem in homesteading access.
2024 - Pilot Launch: First 32 members joined the Community Land Trust.
2025 - Digital Platform: Launched marketplace and workshop registration.
2026 - Expansion: Scaling to more municipalities in Batangas, Laguna, and Quezon.
