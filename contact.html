How to Get to RRJ FARM Lipa City

From Manila (Bus)

Take a bus from Buendia or Cubao going to Lipa City (ALPS, JAM, or DLTB). Travel time: 1.5-2 hours. Fare: ₱200-250. From Lipa Grand Terminal, take a tricycle to RRJ FARM (₱50).

From Manila (Private Car)

Take SLEX to STAR Tollway, exit at Lipa City. Follow signs to Lipa City proper. Waze/Google Maps: "RRJ FARM Lipa City". Parking available on-site.

From Batangas City

Take a jeepney or van from Batangas Grand Terminal to Lipa City. Travel time: 45 minutes. Fare: ₱80-100.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for land stewardship? ▼
You can apply through our website! Go to the Home page and fill out the "Apply for Land Stewardship" form. A CLT administrator will contact you within 3 business days.
What are the CLT fees? ▼
CLT fees range from ₱3,000 to ₱9,000 per month depending on farm size (0.5 to 2 hectares). This includes land maintenance, property tax, and access to shared infrastructure.
Can I visit RRJ FARM before applying? ▼
Yes! We encourage farm visits. Please schedule an appointment through our contact form or call 0912 345 6789. Farm tours are available every Saturday at 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.
Do I need farming experience to join? ▼
Not at all! RRJ FARM welcomes beginners. We offer hands-on workshops and mentorship programs. Many of our successful members started with zero experience.
How do I sell products on the marketplace? ▼
Go to our Marketplace page and fill out the "Sell Your Product" form. Your listing will appear immediately. You can deliver products to our Lipa City hub.
Is there parking available? ▼
Yes, we have free parking spaces available for members and visitors at both our Main Office in Brgy. San Celestino and Demo Farm in Brgy. Bulacnin.
